To obtain North-up, East-left on Clio images, you need to rotate your images COUNTERCLOCKWISE by the following:


DEROT = ROTOFF - 180 + NORTH_CLIO


To rotate your images to get North up:
Rotate COUNTER-CLOCKWISE by angle DEROT.

where ROTOFF is the keyword in the header file (= PARANG when the rotator angle = 0).



Get NORTH_CLIO at Astrometric Calibration
