The Clio prism is a direct-vision design prism, which means it has three wedges that make up the overall prism. The undeviated wavelength is 2 microns. Because it is a prism (not a grating) the dispersion is nonlinear.

Spectral Resolution

The spectral resolution as a function of wavelength for a 0.2" image is

Wavelength R (for 0.2" image)
1.65 8
2.2 34
3.5 126
4.0 157
