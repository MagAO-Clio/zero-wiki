Unpacking and Assembly

· Unpacked Clio, Rack crate and cart crate: no damage. Drop and Tells were torn off.

· Assembled Clio and cart and stored empty crates in La Bodega


Electronics

· Assembled 2 ea 15 ft power cables and 2ea 15 ft coolant hoses. Red is Feed, Blue is return

· Sealed the umbilical port on Rack

· Used spacer and tie wraps to secure motor D connectors on CLIO so they will not accidentally come off during operation

· We changed the O-rings to Viton for cooling line in Rack

· Transported the Rack to the Telescope, connected the coolant hoses, connected power and turned on the pump. There were no leaks and the rack had the usual vibration. There was no vibration on platform (used hands).

· Returned the Rack to the clean room and continued with Clio testing

· Assembled Grounding straps with eyelets for controller grounding to Clio and tied wire bundles on Clio


Testing

· Confirmed that detector worked at room temperature worked at room temperature
