Prepare the instrument for transport

  1. Turn off the computer in rack (if rack is on)
  2. Unplug electronics rack from the AC outlet, if connected.
  3. Disconnect all fibers, cables, hoses, AC from Clio cryostat.
    1. Disconnect orange fiber from gold box.
    2. Disconnect AC cord from grey box.
    3. Remove tube from the solid nitrogen vessel
    4. Unscrew two screws that hold the cable strain relief to Clio cryostat.

  4. Coil the black umbilical cable on the lectronics rack.
  5. Check for any loose equipment in rack or on cryostat cart.

Clio is now ready to be transported to the telescope.

Transport to the Telescope Platform

  1. Transport electronics rack and cryostat to telescope on flatbed truck.
    1. Secure to equipment for transport via straps.
  2. Transfer the equipment to the Nasmyth platform via elevator
  3. Place electronics rack to the left of the Nasmyth ring, away from the telescope.

Attach Cryostat to the Telescope

  1. Position cryostat in front of the NAS assembly
  2. Screw in two Clio pins on NAS assembly at 12 o'clock and 6 o'clock position.
  3. Attach lifting straps to cryostat at its two lift points
  4. Loosen two knobs on Clio cart to allow cryostat to be lifted off the cart
  5. Use crane to lift the cryostat
  6. Remove the window cover before installing (Caution: exposed glass)
  7. Guide cryostat to align with the two pins
  8. Gently slide cryostat on the pins until the ring is flush with the NAS assembly.
  9. Install X bolts on the ring. Pins can be left in place.
  10. Torque all bolts to 55 ft.-lbs. (74 N-m)
  11. Release cryostat from crane and remove straps.

Attach Cables to Instrument and Facility

  1. Hook up cooling to the electronics rack
  2. Plug in network cable from the electronics rack.
  3. Plug in clean and dirty power to the electronics rack.
    1. White is dirty power (Direct at LCO). Yellow is clean power (UPS at LCO).
  4. Uncoil black umbilical cord from electronics rack and route to cryostat.
  5. Attach triangular strain relief assembly to the post at the back of cryostat with two screws.
  6. Plug in circular connector containing home/limits and temperature lines.
  7. Plug in orange fiber to gold SDSU controller box.
  8. Plug in AC cord to SDSU power supply (grey box).
  9. Attach clear vacuum hose to inner solid N2 vessel with hose clamp.
  10. Plug in the 6 motor cables from the umbilical cord to the stepper motor cables on the cryostat.
