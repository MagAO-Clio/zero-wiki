Diagrams of the Clio Pupil Wheel

  • Measurements:
    • Designed in 2004 by IR Labs
    • Tolerance on wheel: 0.005 inch "5 thou" probably.
    • 100 micron tolerance in alignment
    • Center of rotation to center of optic in slot = 1.12 inches
    • Diameter of wheel: 3.447 inches (inner); 3.531 inches (outer)
    • Slot diameter: 1.005 inches (outer); 0.875 inches (inner); Thus the lip/shelf is 1.65mm deep.
    • Spec given for the NRM mask: 1.00 inch plus 0.0 minus 0.002
    • f/16, so 1-inch field stop opening (2.54 mm) is about 50'' FoV Narrow cam
    • Narrow Cam Beam Dimensions:
      • Outer diam: 3.32 mm
      • Inner diam: 0.97 mm
    • Narrow Cam Beam Undersized for vAPP with 100um alignment tol:
      • Outer diam: 3.22 mm
      • Inner diam: 1.07 mm
      • This equals a 33.2% obscuration
    • Narrow Cam Cold Stop:
      • Outer circle 3.32 mm
      • Inner circle 0.96 mm
    • Wide Cam Cold Stop:
      • Outer circle 4.33 mm
      • Inner circle 1.25 mm
      • Spiders 0.1? mm width
